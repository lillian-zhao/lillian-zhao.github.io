♥ Characters ♥

One of the most interesting aspects of Honkai Impact 3rd are the characters and battlesuits which all have their own unique skills, play types, and backstories.

In the context of the game, these characters are refered to as Valkyries which you can gacha for and play as!

While there are only 28 unique Valykries, many have numerous battlesuits, leading to a multitude of unique combinations when playing and a grand total of 89 battlesuits with new ones being added all the time.

Below is a Chart of some (but not all) of the characters and their features to help you get started!

Good Luck!
